## FAQ

### Is 10 fixes per month really enough?

For most small stores, yes. Our scanner prioritizes the most critical violations first — things like missing form labels, missing alt text on product images, and broken keyboard navigation. Many stores resolve their top 10 issues and achieve baseline WCAG 2.1 AA compliance without upgrading. If you have a large catalog or complex checkout, Pro gives you unlimited fixes to cover everything.

### What exactly counts as a "fix"?

Each automated correction applied to your site — adding alt text, fixing contrast ratios, repairing keyboard focus order, adding ARIA labels, and more. A single product page might have 5–12 violations that each count as one fix.

### Will these fixes break my theme or my checkout?

No. Our fixes are injected via WordPress hooks and don't modify your theme files. They persist through theme updates. We test against the 50 most popular WooCommerce themes. If you hit an edge case, our support team will sort it out.

### I already have a developer. Why would I pay for this?

Because your developer shouldn't be spending hours manually adding ARIA labels. WooCommerce Accessibility Fixer handles the routine violations automatically — freeing your developer for work that actually needs them. For agencies, the white-label reports and API access make it easy to upsell accessibility as a premium service to your clients.

### Do you cover ADA and WCAG compliance, not just WCAG?

WCAG 2.1 AA is the standard that underpins ADA web compliance. U.S. courts have consistently used WCAG 2.1 AA as the benchmark for ADA compliance. Our fixes address the full WCAG 2.1 AA checklist. We can't guarantee legal outcomes (no tool can), but we significantly reduce your exposure.

### Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.
